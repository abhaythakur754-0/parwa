# Privacy Policy
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-11

Welcome to **PARWA**. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our AI workforce platform, or interact with our services. 

By using PARWA, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information that identifies, relates to, describes, or is reasonably capable of being associated with a particular consumer or device ("Personal Information").

### A. Information You Provide to Us
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, password, company name, and related contact details.
- **Billing Information:** Credit card details and billing address (processed securely via Stripe).
- **Support Data:** Information you provide when contacting our support team or opening a ticket.

### B. Information We Collect Automatically
- **Usage Data:** IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, pages viewed, and the referring website address.
- **Cookies & Tracking:** We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our platform and hold certain information.

### C. Information Collected via AI Interactions
PARWA operates autonomous AI agents (Mini PARWA, PARWA, PARWA High). When you or your customers interact with these agents, we collect:
- **Chat Logs:** Transcripts of text-based conversations with our AI agents.
- **Voice Recordings:** Audio recordings and transcripts of voice calls handled by PARWA High or other voice-enabled variants.
- **Contextual Data:** Information provided during the session needed to resolve queries (e.g., order numbers, account IDs).

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various business purposes, including to:
- **Provide and Maintain the Service:** Operate the PARWA platform, route queries via the Smart Router, and execute AI agent workflows.
- **Improve our AI Models:** Chat logs and voice transcripts are retained and used explicitly for our **Agent Lightning** weekly self-improvement training cycle.
- **Process Payments:** Securely handle subscription and usage billing via Stripe.
- **Communicate with You:** Send technical notices, security alerts, support messages, and administrative updates.
- **Ensure Security & Compliance:** Monitor against fraudulent activity, verify identity, and comply with legal obligations.

---

## 3. Data Retention

We retain Personal Information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:
- **Account Data:** Retained for the duration of your active subscription, plus any legally required period thereafter.
- **Chat Logs & Voice Recordings:** Retained for the ongoing training of the Agent Lightning system unless explicitly requested for deletion (subject to account tier agreements).
- **Audit Trails:** Certain system interactions and financial recommendations (e.g., refund approvals) are logged immutably for compliance purposes.

---

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your Personal Information. We share it only in the following circumstances:

### A. With Sub-Processors
We employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service. These sub-processors have access to your Personal Information only to perform these tasks on our behalf:
- **OpenRouter:** For routing AI model requests (Light, Heavy, Ultra tiers).
- **Stripe:** For payment processing and billing.
- **Twilio:** For handling voice calls, SMS, and telecommunications infrastructure.
- **Supabase:** For database hosting and secure data storage.

### B. For Legal Reasons
We may disclose your information where required to do so by law or subpoena or if we believe that such action is necessary to comply with the law and the reasonable requests of law enforcement.

---

## 5. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights regarding your Personal Information:

- **Right to Access:** You have the right to request copies of your Personal Information.
- **Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten):** You have the right to request the deletion of your Personal Information (including chat/voice logs), subject to certain exceptions.
- **Right to Data Portability:** You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you.
- **Right to Rectification:** You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at `privacy@parwa.ai`.

## 6. Security of Your Data

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your Personal Information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide (including encryption at rest and secure sub-processor chains), please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.
